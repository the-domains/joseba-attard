Olabe (Bedarona, Basque Country)

"Olabe" is a 400 year old farmhouse located in Bedarona (Ea), on the Basque coast. We have recently bought the property with a vision to see it renovated, that it would become a cultural hub for people from all walks of life.

A Leap of Faith

Sometimes life requires taking some big risks, to jump into something where you're not sure of the outcome. Some people call it foolishness, Jack Kerouac might call it crazy. We're taking that jump, leaving the security of "the shire" behind us. Quite simply, to us, it is a leap of faith.
